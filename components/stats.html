Stat blocks, Stat bars, Factoids and Rankings

Stat blocks, Stat bars, Factoids and Rankings are elements that include large number value and a description and horizontal or vertical bars on one side. It can be static or a link. Stat bars are similar to Stat Blocks but wider. Factoids are used for statements that don't begin with a prominent number or symbol ($, %). Rankings are used for statements that do begin with a prominent number or symbol ($, #).
